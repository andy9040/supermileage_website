Who We Are
We're all University of Michigan students, who work in our spare time to create a car that is as efficient as possible.
What Do We Do?
During the first part of each academic year, we design a high-efficiency car, and then throughout the rest of the year leading up to competition, we build our design.
How Do We Compete?
Our team competes in the Shell Eco-Marathon, a competition started by Shell employees years ago to see who could build the car that consumes the least gasoline.
