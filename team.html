Meet the Teams

Body

Our Body Team is responsible for designing and building the carbon fiber bodies of our vehicles.

Skills Used

  • Materials Science
  • Creating molds for vehicle chassis and top
  • Vacuum infusion
  • Spray painting
  • Using CAD software to design vehicle body
  • …and more practical engineering skills!

Electric Motor

Our Electric Motor Team engineers the electric systems of our vehicles.

Skills Used

  • Knowledge of electronics, components, electric motors, and transmission systems
  • Design and optimize multi-layer printed circuit board (PCB) layouts in Altium Designer
  • Simulate sub circuits with LTSpice
  • Design and optimize mounting systems for electrical systems and drivetrain in NX
  • Hands-on manufacturing & assembly skills
  • Develop C code to optimize vehicle performance and implement a field-oriented control system
  • …and more practical engineering skills!

Internal Combustion Engine

Our Internal Combustion Engine Team is responsible for designing and building the power system for our internal combustion engine-powered vehicle.

Skills Used

  • Machining (drill press, band saw, mill, lathe)
  • CAD
  • Engine control unit programming (MATLAB and PiSnoop)
  • Engine functionality and basic optimization
  • Basic electronics and sensor setup and testing
  • …and more practical engineering skills!

Mechanical Systems

Our Mechanical Systems Team is responsible for various systems that the driver interacts with within the car including the steering system, braking system, and safety features.

Skills Used

  • CAD
  • Manufacturing on lathe, mill & water jet
  • 3D printing
  • Vehicle design concepts (steering, propulsion, systems meshing)
  • Fun, niche topics like bicycle wheel construction, design optimization, and user-based design
  • …and more practical engineering skills!

Public Relations

Our Public Relations Team spreads awareness about our team and keeps our supporters and our sponsors updated on the progress on our team.

Skills Used

  • Graphic Design
  • Communication with Sponsors
  • Public outreach
  • Creating content for our social media accounts
  • Creative writing
  • Fundraising
  • Learning how to promote an engineering organization to the general public

Race Strategy

Our Race Strategy Team optimizes our performance at competition.

Skills Used

  • Performance optimized design
  • Design simulation
  • CAD
  • Arduino
  • Machining (drill press, band saw, mill, lathe)
  • Engineering optimization
  • … and more practical engineering skills!

Vehicle Integration

Our Vehicle Integration Team works with other subteams to ensure reliability between our interconnected systems before our drivers enter the vehicle.

Skills Used

  • Technical communication between subteams
  • In depth knowledge of the entire car & its relationship to the different way systems interact with each other
  • Proficiency in NX & SolidWorks CAD design software
  • Knowledge of proper revision control through Teamcenter
  • Milling and manufacturing of aluminum and carbon fiber car components
  • Task delegation & time management of subteam
